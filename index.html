Inference rules - Armstrong's Axiom
  1. Reflexivity -
    If {B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m } ⊆ {A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n },
    then A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n → B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m . This is called trivial FD's.
  2. Augmentation -
    If A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n → B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m ,
    then A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n C 1 , C 2 , ..., C k → B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m C 1 , C 2 , ..., C k for any set of attributes C 1 , C 2 , ..., C k
  3. Transitivity -
    If A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n → B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m and B 1 , B 2 , ..., B m → C 1 , C 2 , ..., C k ,
    then A 1 , A 2 , ..., A n → C 1 , C 2 , ..., C k
